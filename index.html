Overview

Legacy isn’t built by accident. It begins with vision clear, compelling, and deeply anchored in values.

In a world driven by trends, speed, and survival mode, many families drift without direction. But not yours.

"You’re called to build deliberately, to parent prophetically, and to lead with purpose. "

This free webinar will show you why vision isn’t a luxury, it is necessity and how to shape one that your children can run with.

What You Will Learn

Family Vision

What "family vision" really means and why most families don't have one.

Family Types

The 4 types of families and how they shape their legacy by default or by design.

Core Values

How to clarify your family's core values, purpose, and long-term direction.

Vision Protection

Why vision protects your children from identity confusion and cultural conformity.

Involving Children

How to involve your children in the vision, even if they're young.

Family Rhythms

Daily, weekly, and seasonal rhythms that reinforce your legacy vision.
